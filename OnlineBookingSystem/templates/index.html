7-Day Psychology

Established in 2010 and located in the inner west, the 7 Day Psychology Centre is a network of experienced clinical psychologists providing total help for the individual, the partner and the parent.

Our clinical psychologists have expertise in adult, adolescent and child disorders. They are all trained in treating depression and anxiety disorders and specialise in a variety of other areas. All our clinical psychologists are registered with the Psychology Board of Australia and endorsed for clinical psychology as an approved area of practice.
